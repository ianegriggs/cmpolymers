CM Polymers is a family owned and operated polymers broker specializing in post industrial plastic scrap. We are always looking to buy, so if you're looking to sell don't hesitate to reach out.


Carl N. Myers - President

Lancaster Pennsylvania born and bred Carl Myers is a 35 year veteran of the Plastics Industry. Following an over decade-long stint at Union Carbide, Carl founded CM Polymers in 1993. He is a graduate of Penn State University with a degree in Chemical Engineering, as well as an M.B.A. from Seton Hall. Fun Fact: An avid horseracing enthusiast, Carl is a member of the ownership syndicate for the 2013 Belmont Stakes winning thoroughbred, Palace Malice.



Cory Myers - Director of Sales and Marketing

Since graduating from Muhlenberg College with a degree in Political Science, Cory now works in different kind of Poly Sci. A hardcore NASCAR fan, this young buck is coming up hot in the plastics world. Fun Fact: In addition to his role as CM Polymers' Director of Sales and Marketing, Cory also serves as the acting Dean of Admissions at CM Poly State University - an interactive online campus devoted to the universal betterment of the Polymers Industry Community.
